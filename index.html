About Rob Marston

Jack of All Trades.
Master of Systems.

Rob Marston is the founder and lead of GoldShore LLC — a boutique agency at the intersection of cybersecurity, AI engineering, and product design. With a career spanning CAD engineering, enterprise IT, full-stack development, and AI research, Rob brings rare depth across disciplines.

His philosophy: build things that last. Whether architecting a risk control system for a fintech firm or designing a high-conversion ecommerce platform, Rob approaches every project with the same sharp eye for systems thinking, user experience, and long-term maintainability.

Based on Long Island, NY, GoldShore serves clients locally and nationally — bringing Silicon Valley-caliber solutions to markets that demand reliability, discretion, and results.
